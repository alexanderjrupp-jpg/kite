🪁 Kite — Privacy & Data Policy

Effective July 8, 2026 · Contact: alexanderjrupp@gmail.com

Kite is a free communication (AAC) app built by a family, for families. Our privacy position is simple: your child's voice belongs to your family. We don't run ads, we don't use trackers, and we will never sell or share your data with anyone. This page explains exactly what Kite stores and why.

What Kite stores, and where

On your device: the communication board (words, pages, photos, voice recordings), settings, and a private usage log (which words were tapped, and when) used only to show you progress reports. This all lives on the device itself and works fully offline.

In the cloud (only if you turn on sync): the same board content is stored in a Google Firebase database under your random 8-character family code, so your family's devices stay in step. Usage counts (words per day) sync so a parent's phone can show reports.

If you create an optional account: we store your email address and your family code in a private profile, used for exactly one purpose — restoring your board if you lose your code or device. Accounts are optional; Kite works without one.

Children's privacy

Kite is used by children, but it is set up and controlled entirely by a parent or guardian. Kite never asks a child for personal information. Any photos, names, or voice recordings on a board are added by the parent, for their own family, in their own family's storage space. Speech is generated on the device — nothing your child says is sent anywhere or recorded by us. Practice games and reports run privately on the device.

Who can see your board

Anyone who has your family code can view and join your family's board — that's how parents, grandparents, and teachers connect. Treat the code like a house key and share it only with your child's team. Your account profile (email + code) is readable only by you.

Deleting your data

You can delete board content anytime in the app (Edit mode), disconnect any device (Settings → Account & sync), or reset a device to a blank starter board. For complete removal of your family's cloud data and account, email alexanderjrupp@gmail.com from your account email and it will be permanently deleted.

Security & the fine print

All cloud traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and stored on Google's infrastructure; access requires authentication. No system is perfectly secure — we minimize what we store so there's little to lose. Symbols are © ARASAAC (Government of Aragón, Spain), used under CC BY-NC-SA. Kite is a communication support tool, not a medical device, and doesn't replace guidance from a speech-language professional. Kite is provided free, as-is, without warranty.
